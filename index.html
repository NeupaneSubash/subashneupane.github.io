Teaching

Spring
CS 5310 · Meharry Medical College

Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

An introduction to AI and machine learning techniques applied to clinical problems, including NLP for clinical notes, predictive modeling, and ethical considerations in healthcare AI.

Goal → Students will design and evaluate a prototype AI tool that addresses a real-world clinical workflow challenge.
Graduate 3 Credits
Summer
CS 4250 · Meharry Medical College

Natural Language Processing

Theory and practice of NLP including text classification, named entity recognition, transformer models, and applications in information retrieval and clinical text mining.

Goal → Students will build an end-to-end NLP pipeline that processes unstructured clinical text into structured, actionable outputs.
Graduate 3 Credits
Fall
CS 5420 · Meharry Medical College

Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Covers foundational principles of cybersecurity including intrusion detection, adversarial ML, network security, and emerging GenAI-enabled defense strategies.

Goal → Students will analyze a real-world cyber incident, design a detection pipeline, and present a post-mortem report with mitigation strategies.
Undergraduate / Graduate 3 Credits
